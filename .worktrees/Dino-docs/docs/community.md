# Community

Welcome to the DINOForge community! This is the hub for connecting with other modders, sharing your work, and helping shape the future of DINO modding.

## GitHub Discussions

Our primary community space is [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/KooshaPari/Dino/discussions). Here you can:

### Ask Questions
Have a question about DINOForge or DINO modding? Post in [Q&A](https://github.com/KooshaPari/Dino/discussions/categories/q-a). The community and maintainers are here to help.

### Showcase Your Mods
Share your completed mod packs in [Pack Showcase](https://github.com/KooshaPari/Dino/discussions/categories/pack-showcase). Include:
- Pack name and version
- Download link or repository URL
- A description of what it does
- Screenshots or video clips (optional)

This is the best way to get exposure for your work and inspire other modders.

### Share Ideas
Have a feature request or improvement idea? Post in [Ideas](https://github.com/KooshaPari/Dino/discussions/categories/ideas). Tell us:
- What you want to build or improve
- Which area it affects (SDK, Runtime, Tooling, Docs, Community, etc.)
- Why it would be valuable

Community votes and maintainer feedback help us prioritize the roadmap.

### Stay Updated
Follow [Announcements](https://github.com/KooshaPari/Dino/discussions/categories/announcements) for official updates about releases, breaking changes, and platform milestones.

## Reporting Issues

Found a bug or critical issue? Please report it on [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/KooshaPari/Dino/issues) with as much detail as possible:
- What you were trying to do
- What happened
- What you expected to happen
- Steps to reproduce
- Your environment (Windows/Linux, .NET version, etc.)

We have templates to help guide your report.

## Contributing

Want to contribute code, documentation, or tooling? See [CONTRIBUTING.md](https://github.com/KooshaPari/Dino/blob/main/CONTRIBUTING.md) for:
- How to set up a development environment
- Code style and commit conventions
- Pull request process
- Testing requirements

## Community Guidelines

Please be respectful and constructive in all interactions. We follow the [Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct](https://github.com/KooshaPari/Dino/blob/main/CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md). In brief:
- **Be inclusive** - Welcome all skill levels and backgrounds
- **Be helpful** - Share knowledge generously
- **Be respectful** - Disagree constructively
- **Be on-topic** - Keep discussions focused on DINOForge and DINO modding
