## 1. BUILDINGS

### Resource Gathering Buildings

#### Lumber Mill
- **Tier 1**: Produces Wood
- **Production**: Harvests wood from resource nodes

#### Stone Mine
- **Tier 1**: Produces Stone
- **Production**: Harvests stone from resource nodes

#### Farm
- **Tier 1**: Produces Food via agriculture

#### Fisherman's Hut
- **Tier 1**: Produces Food via fishing
- **Workers**: 2 workers
- **Production**: 3x slower than berry picker but consistent
- **Note**: Fish are always at same spot, reducing travel time

#### Berry Picker's House
- **Tier 1**: Produces Food via berry collection
- **Workers**: 1 worker
- **Travel Time**: Must travel to exhausted berry bushes

#### Iron Mine
- **Requires**: Town Hall Level 2
- **Produces**: Iron

#### Gold Mine
- **Produces**: Gold (for late-game economy)

### Military Production Buildings

#### Barracks I
- **Produces**: Swordsmen, Archers, basic infantry
- **Unit Cost**: 1 citizen per unit
- **Research**: Mongoose Reflexes, Sharpshooter

#### Barracks II
- **Requires**: Town Hall II
- **Produces**: Spearmen, Crossbowmen, Healers
- **Research**: Quad Cure, Harsh Training, Quick Reload

#### Barracks III
- **Requires**: Town Hall III
- **Produces**: Foot Knights, advanced infantry
- **Research**: Blacksmith Guild, Infected Mushroom, Cast-Iron Hammer

#### Stables I
- **Requires**: Town Hall II
- **Produces**: Basic cavalry (Horse Archers)

#### Stables II/III
- **Requires**: Town Hall III
- **Produces**: Mounted Knights, heavy cavalry

#### Engineer Guild I
- **Produces**: Catapults
- **Research**: Conveyor Method, Big Rocks

#### Engineer Guild II
- **Produces**: Trebuchets, Ballistae
- **Research**: Manufacturing Production, Shrapnel Projectiles, Foolproof Charge

### Defense Buildings

#### Wooden Gate, Stone Gate, Wall
- **Types**: Wooden and stone variants for defense

#### Tower
- **Type**: Defensive structure supporting ranged units

#### Stone Obelisk
- **Type**: Defensive structure

#### Undead Tower (Undead Faction)
- **New in**: Undead update

#### Massive Undead Tower (Undead Faction)
- **New in**: Undead update

### Economy Buildings

#### Town Hall
- **Level 1**: Base structure, research hub
  - Food Storage: 100 units
- **Level 2 (150 Wood + 100 Stone)**: Unlocks iron mining, Stables I
- **Level 3**: Unlocks Level 3 buildings, advanced units

#### Granary
- **Purpose**: Expands food storage

#### Storage Building
- **Purpose**: Expands Wood, Stone, Iron shared storage
- **Storage Bonuses**: Wood Cart (+40), Stone Cart (+15), Food Cart (+20)

#### Market
- **Requires**: Town Hall II for gold transactions
- **Purpose**: Trading and gold acquisition

#### House I
- **Cost**: 25 Wood
- **Capacity**: 6 citizens

#### House II
- **Cost**: 25 Wood + 14 Stone
- **Capacity**: 12 citizens

#### House III
- **Capacity**: 18 citizens
- **Requires**: Town Hall II

### Special Buildings

#### Hospital
- **Purpose**: Medical care and disease management
- **Research**: Hygiene, Urgency Bonus, General Wards

#### University
- **Purpose**: Advanced research and citizen welfare
- **Research**: Dietetics, Urban Planning I, Urban Planning II

#### Rebel Cabin
- **Purpose**: Magic spell access via Soul Crystals
- **Faction**: Rebel

#### Death Metal Mine (Undead)
- **Produces**: Death Metal (unique undead resource)

#### Death Metal Storage (Undead)
- **Stores**: Death Metal resource

#### Dealer Worm (Undead)
- **Purpose**: Resource trading/exchange

## 2. UNITS & COMBAT

### Infantry
- **Swordsman**: 55 HP, 5 DPS, Light armor, Barracks I
- **Axeman**: 80 HP, 10 DPS, Light armor, Barracks I-II
- **Foot Knight**: 300 HP, 9.75 DPS, Heavy armor (70-100% reduction), Barracks III
- **Spearman**: 100 HP, 6 DPS + 300% vs cavalry, Medium armor, -50% arrow damage, +3 HP/sec, Barracks II

### Ranged
- **Archer**: 5 DPS (ranged), Light armor, Barracks I
- **Crossbowman**: Higher damage than Archer, Extended range, Barracks II
- **Horse Archer**: Mobile ranged, Stables I

### Cavalry
- **Mounted Knight**: 400 HP, 13 DPS, Heavy armor, Stables III

### Support & Siege
- **Healer**: Healing support, Barracks II (weak healing)
- **Hammerguy**: 8 DPS area damage, Heavy armor, Barracks III
- **Catapult**: Cheap area weapon, no iron requirement, Engineer Guild I
- **Ballista**: Direct fire siege, Engineer Guild II
- **Trebuchet**: Parabolic fire siege, Engineer Guild II

### Summoned
- **Dark Knight**: 2 Soul Crystals summon 5 units for 120 seconds

### Undead Units
- Hunting Fiend, Flesh Collector, Thundering Amalgam, Drake variants, Undead Horseman

## 3. ECONOMY SYSTEM

### Resources
- **Food**: Consumed 1/citizen/day; sources: Farms, Fisherman's Hut, Berry Picker
- **Wood**: Limited early, abundant late
- **Stone**: Defense and advanced buildings
- **Iron**: Requires Town Hall II; precious
- **Gold**: Trade-only via Market (Town Hall II required)
- **Soul Crystal**: Rebel magic system
- **Death Metal**: Undead faction only

### Population & Housing
- **Growth Rate**: Citizens added daily if housing exists
- **House I**: 6 capacity, 25 Wood
- **House II**: 12 capacity, 25 Wood + 14 Stone
- **House III**: 18 capacity, requires Town Hall II

### Storage
- **Town Hall**: 100 Food; shared Wood/Stone/Iron pool
- **Cart Bonuses**: Wood +40, Stone +15, Food +20
- **Shared Pool**: Reaching limit blocks ALL three resources

### Trading & Commerce
- **Market**: Exchange resources for gold
- **Gold**: Critical for late-game

## 5. FACTIONS

### Lord's Troops
- **Playstyle**: Traditional RTS, balanced
- **Defense**: Stone walls
- **Units**: Swordsmen, Archers, Crossbowmen, Knights, Cavalry
- **Tech**: Full standard tree

### Rebels
- **Playstyle**: Magic-focused, summoning
- **Defense**: Wooden structures only
- **Magic**: Soul Crystal spellcasting
- **Spells**: Summon (2, 5 Dark Knights, 120s), Astral Ray (5), Mass Healing (2, 100 HP), Meteor (10, 500 dmg)
- **Tactics**: Area buffs, weak units, mass infantry
- **Unique**: Rebel Cabin

### Sarranga (Tribes)
- **Playstyle**: Aggressive, mobile, champion-focused
- **Strength**: Champions with ranged support
- **Tech**: "Ancient" upgrade level available

### Undead
- **Playstyle**: Offensive, corpse-based, resurrection
- **Defense**: No walls; "rows of bodies"
- **Economy**: Death Metal resource
- **Core**: Kill enemies → raise zombies → recycle to bones
- **Units**: Hunting Fiend, Flesh Collector, Thundering Amalgam, Drake variants, Undead Horseman
- **Tech Tree**: 64 exclusive technologies (2025 update)
- **Buildings**: Death Metal Mine, Death Metal Storage, Dealer Worm, Undead Towers

## 6. SPECIAL MECHANICS

### Combat
- **Armor Types**: Light, Medium, Heavy (70-100% reduction)
- **Spearmen**: +300% vs cavalry, -50% arrows, +3 HP/sec regen
- **Area Damage**: Hammerguy, siege weapons
- **Avoidance**: Swordsmen with Mongoose +15%

### Magic (Rebel)
- **Soul Crystals**: Spell resource
- **Spells**: Summon, Astral Ray, Mass Healing, Meteor

### Undead Mechanics
- **Corpse Recycling**: Kill → zombies → bones
- **Necromancy**: Raise fallen units temporarily
- **Death Metal Economy**: Parallel resource
- **Aggressive Incentive**: Victory fuels economy

### Production
- **Units**: 1 citizen per unit
- **Research**: One at a time, 1-4 minutes
- **Buildings**: Variable with builders and speed research
