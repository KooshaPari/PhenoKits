# Observably Brand Identity

## Overview
**Observably** — A comprehensive observability and telemetry framework for understanding distributed systems at scale.

**Tagline:** See everything, understand it all.

## Color Palette

| Role | Hex | Usage |
|------|-----|-------|
| Accent | #06B6D4 | Primary interactive elements, CTAs, highlights |
| Neutral | #6B7280 | Body text, secondary UI, backgrounds |

## Typography

- **Monospace** (code, technical UI): IBM Plex Mono, Roboto Mono, or system monospace
- **Body** (prose, labels): Inter, Helvetica Neue, or system sans-serif

## Visual Identity

**Logo Placeholder:** `assets/logo-placeholder.svg` (256x256 SVG, uppercase "O" in cyan)

**Symbol Rationale:** The letter "O" represents *observation* and the cyclical nature of observability: instrument → collect → analyze → iterate. The bright cyan (#06B6D4) conveys clarity, visibility, and technical precision. It suggests light cutting through the fog of distributed systems—the core promise of observability.

## Brand Voice

Observably's voice is technical, direct, and empowering. We address operators, SREs, and platform engineers who need to trust their systems. Copy emphasizes transparency, completeness, and actionability: not just gathering telemetry, but turning it into insight and confidence.
