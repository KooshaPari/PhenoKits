## 1. Mission Statement

### 1.1 Primary Mission

**PhenoSpec is the specification management and standards platform for the Phenotype ecosystem, providing specification templates, validation rules, and standard definitions that ensure consistency and quality across all Phenotype specifications.**

Our mission is to standardize specifications by offering:
- **Spec Templates**: Standardized formats
- **Validation Rules**: Spec quality checks
- **Standard Library**: Reusable definitions
- **Review Process**: Quality gates

### 1.2 Vision

To be the spec authority where:
- **Specs are Consistent**: Standard format
- **Quality is High**: Validated inputs
- **Reuse is Common**: Standard components
- **Process is Clear**: Defined workflow

### 1.3 Strategic Objectives

| Objective | Target | Timeline |
|-----------|--------|----------|
| Spec coverage | 100% features | 2026-Q3 |
| Template library | 20+ templates | 2026-Q4 |
| Validation pass rate | >95% | 2026-Q2 |
| Review turnaround | <2 days | 2026-Q2 |
