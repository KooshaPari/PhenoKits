# Stashly Brand Identity

## Overview
**Stashly** — A distributed cache and state management framework for ephemeral, multi-tier data persistence.

**Tagline:** Cache, stash, retrieve—instantly.

## Color Palette

| Role | Hex | Usage |
|------|-----|-------|
| Accent | #14B8A6 | Primary interactive elements, CTAs, highlights |
| Neutral | #6B7280 | Body text, secondary UI, backgrounds |

## Typography

- **Monospace** (code, technical UI): IBM Plex Mono, Roboto Mono, or system monospace
- **Body** (prose, labels): Inter, Helvetica Neue, or system sans-serif

## Visual Identity

**Logo Placeholder:** `assets/logo-placeholder.svg` (256x256 SVG, uppercase "S" in teal)

**Symbol Rationale:** The letter "S" echoes *stash* and *store*—the act of setting data aside for fast retrieval. The teal accent (#14B8A6) conveys freshness, flow, and responsive performance. It suggests motion and fluidity: data moving swiftly between tiers and returning on demand.

## Brand Voice

Stashly's voice is energetic and efficiency-focused. We speak to developers optimizing performance and managing state across distributed boundaries. Copy emphasizes speed, simplicity, and the elegance of multi-tier caching without operational burden.
