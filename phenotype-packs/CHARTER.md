## 1. Mission Statement

### 1.1 Primary Mission

**phenotype-packs is the pack management and distribution system for the Phenotype ecosystem, providing pack definitions, versioning, and distribution that enable modular, composable functionality across Phenotype services.**

Our mission is to enable modular composition by offering:
- **Pack Registry**: Centralized pack discovery
- **Pack Format**: Standard pack structure
- **Version Management**: Pack versioning
- **Distribution**: Pack delivery

### 1.2 Vision

To be the pack standard where:
- **Packs are Discoverable**: Easy to find
- **Composition is Simple**: Mix and match
- **Versions are Managed**: Safe updates
- **Distribution is Reliable**: Available everywhere

### 1.3 Strategic Objectives

| Objective | Target | Timeline |
|-----------|--------|----------|
| Pack count | 100+ packs | 2026-Q4 |
| Adoption | 80% services | 2026-Q4 |
| Distribution success | 99.99% | 2026-Q3 |
| Version compliance | 100% | 2026-Q3 |
