## 1. Mission Statement

### 1.1 Primary Mission

**phenotype-skills is the skill framework and registry for AI agents in the Phenotype ecosystem, providing skill definitions, skill composition, and skill execution that enable AI agents to perform complex tasks.**

Our mission is to enable AI capabilities by offering:
- **Skill Registry**: Discoverable AI skills
- **Skill Framework**: Skill development tools
- **Composition**: Combine skills for complex tasks
- **Execution**: Reliable skill execution

### 1.2 Vision

To be the skill standard where:
- **Skills are Discoverable**: Find the right capability
- **Development is Easy**: Simple skill creation
- **Composition is Powerful**: Complex from simple
- **Execution is Reliable**: Always works

### 1.3 Strategic Objectives

| Objective | Target | Timeline |
|-----------|--------|----------|
| Skill count | 200+ skills | 2026-Q4 |
| Agent success | >80% | 2026-Q4 |
| Development time | <2 hours | 2026-Q2 |
| Registry uptime | 99.99% | 2026-Q2 |
