## 1. Project Overview & Objectives

### 1.1 Vision Statement

phenotype-skills is Phenotype's AI skill library - providing reusable, composable skills for AI agents that enable them to interact with Phenotype services, APIs, and data sources.

### 1.2 Mission Statement

To provide a comprehensive library of AI skills that enable autonomous agents to perform complex tasks, interact with systems, and accomplish goals within the Phenotype ecosystem.
